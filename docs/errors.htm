Errors

 

E100

E.g. KeyError: ('E100', 'M:\\Finance\\pypms\\2012-07\\summary-2012-07.xls', 'Expenses', 3, '2840X')

Says that there’s a problem in the summary Excel workbook, sheet expenses, row 3. It can’t lookup job ‘2840X’. Check that the job exists in the jobs table.
